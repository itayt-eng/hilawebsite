About Hila

A Therapist in Los Altos Who Truly Listens

I'm Hila Teller, a Licensed Clinical Social Worker (LCSW) with a private practice in Los Altos, California. I work with adults navigating anxiety, depression, trauma, major life transitions, and the kinds of relational pain that can feel impossible to put into words.

My work is grounded in a psychodynamic approach — which means we go beyond surface-level coping strategies to explore the deeper patterns and experiences shaping your inner life. My hope is that therapy becomes a space where you feel genuinely seen, and where real change becomes possible.

Whether you're in Los Altos, Mountain View, Palo Alto, or anywhere in California via telehealth, I'd love to connect and explore whether we're a good fit.

My Approach

Psychodynamic Therapy: Going Deeper

I practice psychodynamic therapy — a depth-oriented approach that explores how your history, relationships, and unconscious patterns shape who you are today.

Rather than focusing solely on managing symptoms, we work to understand their roots. This deeper understanding creates insight that leads to lasting change — not just relief, but real transformation.

I bring warmth, curiosity, and directness to every session. Our relationship itself becomes part of the healing: a place to experience being truly known.

Common Questions

Things People Often Want to Know

Reaching out for therapy takes courage. Below are the questions I hear most often — I hope they help.

Ask Me Directly

Are you accepting new clients?

+
Yes — I am currently accepting new clients for both in-person therapy in Los Altos, CA and telehealth sessions throughout California. Please reach out to schedule a free 20-minute phone consultation.

Do you accept insurance?

+
I am a private-pay therapist and do not bill insurance directly. However, I provide a monthly superbill (an itemized receipt with all the codes your insurance needs) that you can submit to your carrier for potential out-of-network reimbursement. Many PPO plans reimburse 50–80% of out-of-network therapy costs.

What are your session fees?

+
Please reach out directly to discuss fees. I am happy to answer questions about session rates and provide the information you need to check your out-of-network benefits before we begin.

What is psychodynamic therapy and how is it different?

+
Psychodynamic therapy is an insight-oriented, depth approach. Rather than teaching coping techniques, we explore the underlying causes of your struggles — patterns rooted in early relationships and experiences. Research shows that psychodynamic therapy produces lasting change that continues to deepen even after therapy ends, sometimes called the "sleeper effect."

How long does therapy take?

+
This is entirely personal. Some clients notice meaningful shifts within a few months; others choose to stay in longer-term, exploratory work. We regularly revisit your goals and progress together, and you are always in control of the timeline.

Do you offer telehealth sessions?

+
Yes. I offer HIPAA-compliant video therapy sessions for clients anywhere in California. Many clients in Mountain View, Palo Alto, Sunnyvale, Cupertino, and beyond choose telehealth for its flexibility. The quality and depth of the work is the same as in-person.

What happens in the first session?

+
The first session is an opportunity to tell your story and for me to begin to understand who you are and what brings you to therapy. There's no pressure to share everything at once. I'll ask some questions, but mostly I want to hear what feels most important to you. By the end, we'll have a sense of whether we feel like a good fit for working together.
